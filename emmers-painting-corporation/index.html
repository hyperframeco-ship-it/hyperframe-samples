Our process

Why our paint stays put.

Most painting failures aren't paint failures — they're prep failures. Here's what every Emmers Painting Corporation job includes before a single coat goes on:

  • 1

    Wash & degloss

    Walls cleaned, glossy surfaces sanded so paint actually bonds — not just sits on top.

  • 2

    Patch & caulk

    Every nail hole, hairline crack, and gap filled. Trim re-caulked where it pulled away.

  • 3

    Prime where it counts

    Stain-blocking primer on water marks, bleeding wood, and any drastic color change.

  • 4

    Two true coats

    Cut in, roll, inspect, second coat. No "one-coat" shortcuts on real jobs.
