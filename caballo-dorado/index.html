Our Story

A family kitchen, open to all.

Caballo Dorado opened with a simple idea: a place where the bread is still warm, the wine pours are honest, and the host knows your name by the second visit.

Every dish is made from scratch — sauces simmered for hours, pasta rolled in the back kitchen, vegetables sourced from farms within an hour's drive. We change the menu with the seasons and we make almost everything ourselves.

— The Caballo Dorado family
