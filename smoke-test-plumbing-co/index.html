About Us

Real Plumbers.
Real Results.

My name is Dave Kowalski, and I started Smoke Test Plumbing Co back in 2009 out of a pickup truck and a whole lot of drive. After 10 years working for other plumbing companies across Will County, I knew there was a better way to do things — honest pricing, no upsells, and actually showing up when we say we will.

Today our crew of licensed plumbers covers all of Joliet, Plainfield, Lockport, and the surrounding communities. Whether you're dealing with a burst pipe at 2 AM or planning a kitchen renovation, we treat your home like it's our own.

What We Do

Full-Service Plumbing
For Joliet Homes & Businesses

From minor repairs to major installations, we handle it all. Every job comes with a written warranty and our no-mess guarantee.

🚨

24/7 Emergency Plumbing

Burst pipe, backed-up sewer, or no hot water at midnight? We answer every call and can typically have a tech at your door within 60 minutes anywhere in Joliet.

🔧

Drain Cleaning & Unclogging

Slow drains and blockages don't stand a chance against our professional hydro-jetting and snake equipment. We clear kitchen, bathroom, and main sewer lines fast.

🚿

Water Heater Installation

We install, repair, and replace traditional tank and tankless water heaters. Same-day service available for most units — so you're never stuck with a cold shower.

🏠

Bathroom & Kitchen Remodels

Relocating a sink, adding a new bathroom, or roughing in fixtures for your kitchen renovation? Our remodel plumbing is clean, code-compliant, and built to last.

💧

Leak Detection & Repair

Hidden slab leaks and pinhole leaks can cost thousands if ignored. We use non-invasive detection equipment to find leaks fast and repair them right the first time.

🔩

Sewer Line Services

Video camera inspections, sewer line repairs, and full replacements. We diagnose the problem before we dig — saving you money and keeping your yard intact.
